What Is Dementia?

Dementia is an umbrella term for conditions that affect a personâ€™s memory, thinking, communication, and daily activities. It is not a normal part of ageing, and every personâ€™s experience is unique. Some people may notice small changes at first, while others may find daily tasks more challenging over time.
